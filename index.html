Grade my Essay was created as a reaction to a national crisis that affected nearly every student in the US. Remote learning is slowly becoming the norm, but while there is an abundance of online resources to help with subjects like math and science, there is a surprising small number of websites that offer essay feedback by real people. Our mission is to cultivate writing skills for students everywhere, and not let a crisis subvert the education of the next generation.
